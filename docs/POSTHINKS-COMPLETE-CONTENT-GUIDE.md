## Writing Style Guidelines

### **Tone & Voice:**
- **Professional tapi approachable** - Bukan formal kaku, tapi juga bukan terlalu casual
- **Confident tapi tidak arrogant** - Meyakinkan tapi tidak sombong
- **Direct dan clear** - Tidak fluffy atau overly poetic

### **Language Style:**
- **Direct dan clear:** Tidak fluffy atau overly poetic
- **Number-driven:** Stats, percentages, concrete metrics
- **Action-oriented:** Verbs seperti "Boost", "Drive", "Achieve", "Deliver"
- **Benefit-first:** Focus pada outcome untuk client
- **Professional:** Business language tapi tidak overly formal

### **Key Phrases Used:**
- "Data-Driven Strategies"
- "Real Results"
- "Comprehensive solutions"
- "Tailored to your brand"
- "Actionable insights"
- "Sustained growth"
- "Proven process"

### **Numbers & Metrics:**
- Selalu gunakan angka spesifik, bukan vague
- Contoh: "500+ projects" (bukan "ratusan projects")
- Contoh: "98% satisfaction" (bukan "tingkat satisfaction tinggi")
- Contoh: "150M+ reach" (bukan "jutaan reach")

### **CTA Best Practices:**
- Action-oriented verbs: "Get Started", "View Our Work", "Contact Us"
- Create urgency: "Ready to grow?" "Let's discuss"
- Benefit-driven: "Get Free Consultation", "Achieve Real Results"
